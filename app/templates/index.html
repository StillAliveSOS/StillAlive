Our Mission

Built for Those Who Carry the World Alone

Millions of people — especially women — live and work independently, far from family. When something goes wrong, silence becomes the greatest danger. StillAlive breaks that silence.

We are building a local governance ecosystem: from real-time check-ins to offline routing, to an AI-powered first-responder network connecting police, ambulance, and neighbours.


Our system is designed to be invisible when you're safe — and unstoppable when you're not. Every check-in is a quiet act of care. Every alert is a community that shows up.


"We don't want to beat anyone. We want to make this society so safe that danger has no room to exist."

How It Works

Simple. Quiet. Always Watching.

Four steps that could save a life.

01
📱
Register & Connect
Sign up, add your trusted contacts and default safe location. Your privacy is always protected.
02
🔔
Periodic Check-ins
The app quietly pings you at set intervals. One tap to confirm you're safe. Miss it — and alerts begin.
03
🗺️
Offline Safe Route
No signal? No problem. Locally downloaded maps show the shortest route to your safe location — always.
04
🚨
Coordinated Response
If danger is flagged, nearby members, police, ambulance, and fire services are alerted instantly.

Features

What StillAlive Brings to Life

💓
Live Presence Pulse
Automated check-in pings ensure someone always knows you are safe. If you don't respond, trusted contacts are notified immediately.
📡
Offline Map Routing
Downloadable local maps with shortest-path algorithm to your home location — works even without mobile data or internet.
Coming Soon
🚨
One-Tap SOS
A single press sends your live location, a distress signal, and contact info to local responders and your trusted network.
🏘️
Locality Network
Like Rapido but for safety — anyone in your locality with the app can respond to alerts, forming a human shield of care.

Unified Emergency Dispatch
